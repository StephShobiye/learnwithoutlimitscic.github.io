About us

Learn Without Limits CIC is a parent‑led community interest company based in Wales. We support families navigating the Additional Learning Needs (ALN) system with clear guidance, practical tools, and a welcoming peer community.

Our aims

  • Make the ALN system understandable and fair
  • Equip parents with letter templates and step‑by‑step checklists
  • Build peer networks so nobody has to do this alone
