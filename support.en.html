Support Learn Without Limits CIC

Learn Without Limits CIC is building practical navigation infrastructure for families affected by disability, additional learning needs, school breakdown and fragmented public systems in Wales.

We are an early-stage CIC with live community reach, public information resources, a working digital development model and a clear plan for long-term sustainability.

Targeted support can accelerate the infrastructure, technical capacity and strategic collaborations required for safe, sustainable growth.

Stay Updated Through Our Open Briefings

We run quarterly open-call briefings for people outside our parent carer community who would like to understand how the programme is developing.

These sessions provide updates on our progress, current priorities, emerging themes from the work and ways to get involved. Everyone is welcome to attend.

You can book onto upcoming briefings through our events page. A write-up from our most recent open briefing is also available on our About page.

Sponsor or Collaborate With Us

We welcome sponsors, funders and strategic collaborators who would like to support a defined workstream, visible asset or stage-gated piece of work.

Current sponsorship priorities include:

Technical Leadership and Digital Infrastructure

Support for senior technical leadership would help us strengthen the Parent Guide App, digital governance, version control, data security, accessibility and safe scaling.

This is the core capacity needed to turn lived-experience insight into stable, practical digital tools.

Branded Community Platform

We are working towards an owned, safeguarded community platform so families are not permanently dependent on third-party social media spaces.

Sponsorship could support platform hosting, setup, moderation tools, accessibility configuration and safe community infrastructure.

Accessibility and Inclusive Technical Digital Design

We are looking for support with technical accessibility review, assistive technology testing, inclusive UX design, WCAG-aligned improvements, accessible front-end development and testing with users who rely on alternative access methods.

Welsh-Language Access

We want to grow Welsh-language access carefully and realistically, including Welsh-language review, bilingual communications, Welsh-speaking volunteer development and future Welsh-language community activity.

Quantitative Research, Data Collection, Measurement and Evaluation

We want to strengthen the evidence base around ALN, disability, school breakdown and fragmented service journeys in Wales.

Support could help with quantitative research, data collection, evaluation frameworks, cost-benefit analysis, benefits measurement and impact reporting.

Podcast, Blog and Public Learning Resources

Sponsorship could help strengthen our public learning layer through podcast production, transcripts, captions, blog development and linked resource packs.

Strategic People We Are Looking For

We are interested in hearing from people who can strengthen Learn Without Limits CIC at board, research, language, digital and infrastructure level.

Third CIC Director

We are looking for a third CIC Director who can bring either:

  • digital infrastructure, civic tech, data, accessibility, cyber/security, product development or app/platform governance expertise
  • fundraising, enterprise development, social investment, sponsorship, strategic collaboration or organisational sustainability expertise

This is a voluntary governance role.

The right person will be comfortable with agile, community-informed governance and will help us strengthen our strategic capacity while retaining the responsiveness that makes the model effective.

Quantitative Researcher, Data Analyst or Evaluation Specialist

We are interested in connecting with a quantitative researcher, data analyst or evaluation specialist who may be interested in future research, evaluation or funding opportunities.

We already hold substantial qualitative insight from families across Wales. The missing piece is quantitative evidence: data that can help identify escalation patterns, estimate costs, measure benefits and strengthen the case for earlier practical navigation support.

This is a measurement and evidence-building opportunity, focused on turning lived-experience patterns into measurable evidence.

Welsh Speakers

We are interested in hearing from Welsh speakers who may want to support the gradual development of Welsh-language access.

This is not a request for unpaid translation of the whole programme. We are especially interested in people who understand disability, additional learning needs, parent carer experience, education, community work or digital inclusion.

Funders and Strategic Collaborators

We are keen to speak with funders and collaborators interested in prevention, digital inclusion, disability, education, civic technology, community insight, social investment, Welsh-language access or systems change.

Our model is based on pattern recognition, not representation. We do not claim to speak for all families. Our value lies in recognising recurring patterns in family experience and converting them into practical navigation infrastructure through a structured, continuous feedback loop informed by PRINCE2 Agile principles.

We are particularly interested in support that helps early-stage organisations build infrastructure before they are expected to generate sustainable income.

We are open to collaboration where roles, ownership, responsibilities and public-benefit purpose are clear.

Paid roles will be advertised openly only if and when funding is secured.
