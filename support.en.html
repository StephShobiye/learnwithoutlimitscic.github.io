Support Us

Support Learn Without Limits CIC

Learn Without Limits CIC is building Wales-wide digital and community infrastructure to improve outcomes for children and young people with Additional Learning Needs (ALN) and disabilities.

Our work is preventative. We focus on reducing escalation, exclusion and crisis by providing practical tools, structured guidance and community support before problems grow.

We are currently unfunded and operating at an early infrastructure stage. We have established our governance foundations, safeguarding framework and proof-of-concept digital tools. We are now seeking structured support to enable sustainable, measurable delivery across Wales.

Sponsor or Partner With Us

We work with organisations and individuals who want to support practical, preventative solutions for families navigating education, health and employability pathways.

  • Sponsoring defined programme activity
  • Supporting digital infrastructure development
  • Sponsoring community events
  • Providing professional expertise or technical support
  • Contributing through clearly defined, role-based volunteering

Our volunteer team includes experienced parent carers, safeguarding-trained community moderators, educators, creative professionals, first language Welsh speakers supporting bilingual development, and individuals providing technical support.

All volunteering is structured, role-based and aligned to defined programme outcomes.
