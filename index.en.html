What we do

Practical guidance for ALN Wales

Clear, parent-friendly guidance on the ALN system in Wales, including IDPs, provision, and routes to challenge decisions. We focus on what families can do next, not just what the law says.

Support when school stops working

Guidance and signposting for families facing reduced timetables, attendance pressure, school-related anxiety and EBSA, plus pathways like EOTAS and alternative provision.

Events and community

Online Q&As and local meet-ups, alongside peer support that respects confidentiality, safeguarding, and boundaries. We are building a calmer, more useful alternative to rage-driven platforms.

Why families use us

Grounded in lived experience

We build tools shaped by the lived experience of parents, carers and young adults, with a focus on what genuinely helps.

Built for busy, burnt-out parents

Short, practical steps. Copy-and-paste wording. Clear options. No judgement.

Designed to scale across Wales

We are developing a digital “parent guide” app and a structured knowledge base so support is not dependent on who happens to be online.
