Learn Without Limits CIC

Parent-led support for ALN families in Wales, grounded in lived experience. We provide clear, practical guidance on ALN, IDPs, EOTAS, EBSA, EHE, and school and college inclusion.

What we offer

  • Practical guides and signposting for ALN families across Wales
  • Templates and printables to help you write to schools and local authorities
  • Clear explanations of common pathways: IDP, EOTAS, EBSA and reduced timetables
  • Community support and trusted resources, with safeguarding built in
