Learn Without Limits CIC

Parent-led support for ALN families in Wales, grounded in the lived experience of parents and young adults. We support families of children and young people navigating ALN, IDPs, EOTAS, EBSA and school inclusion.

What we do

We focus on prevention and early support, so families can understand options and reduce escalation. This includes families dealing with attendance pressure, school-related anxiety, and emotionally based school avoidance (EBSA).

  • Practical guidance for ALN in Wales, including the ALN Act and IDP processes
  • Plain-English explainers for families supporting children and young people
  • Peer support and lived experience mentoring
  • Events including online Q and A sessions and local meet-ups
