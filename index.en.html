Learn Without Limits CIC

Parent-led support for ALN families in Wales, grounded in the lived experience of parents and young adults. We support families navigating ALN, IDPs, EOTAS, EBSA, EHE, and school or college inclusion.

What we do

We focus on prevention and early support, so families can understand their options and reduce escalation. This includes families dealing with attendance pressure, school-related anxiety, and emotionally based school avoidance (EBSA).

We also support Elective Home Educators seeking clear information about how to re-enter education through FE College, Training, or Higher Education routes, and every permutation in between.

  • Plain-English guidance on ALN processes, IDPs, reviews, and next steps.
  • Practical tools including checklists, templates, and copy-and-paste wording.
  • Events and workshops designed to be welcoming and accessible.
  • A digital Parent Guide App to make support easier to access across Wales.
