Learn Without Limits CIC

Parent-led support for ALN families in Wales, grounded in the lived experience of parents and young adults. We support families of children and young people navigating ALN, IDPs, EOTAS, EBSA, EHE, and school/college inclusion.

What we do

We focus on prevention and early support, so families can understand options and reduce escalation. This includes families dealing with attendance pressure, school-related anxiety, and emotionally based school avoidance (EBSA). It also includes Elective Home Educators seeking information about how to re-enter the system to attend FE College, Training or Higher Education routes, and every permutation in between.

  • Plain-English guidance on ALN processes, IDPs, reviews and next steps.
  • Practical tools we are building toolkits to include checklists, templates and copy-and-paste wording.
  • Events and workshops designed to be welcoming and accessible.
  • A digital Parent Guide App we are building to make support easier to access across Wales.
