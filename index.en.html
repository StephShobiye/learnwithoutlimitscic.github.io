Practical ALN support for families in Wales

Learn Without Limits CIC is a parent-led organisation providing clear, practical support for families navigating Additional Learning Needs (ALN) in Wales. We help families understand ALN rights, IDPs, EOTAS, EBSA, EHE, school difficulties, and college transition pathways.

Parent-led • Wales-wide • Practical guidance • Bilingual access

Our Prevent → Bridge → Progress model

We support families earlier, before problems escalate. Our approach focuses on preventing avoidable crisis, bridging periods where education has begun to break down, and supporting progression into education, training, and longer-term outcomes.

Prevent

Early guidance before challenges escalate.

Bridge

Support during school breakdown and crisis.

Progress

We help families understand pathways forward. Progress is delivered by schools, colleges, and statutory services.
