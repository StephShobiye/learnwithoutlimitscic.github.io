Events

Join us

Our sessions are designed to help you feel less alone, understand what’s going on, and work out what to do next.

Most of our events are online, so you can join from home, wherever you are in Wales.

View upcoming sessions on Eventbrite

No pressure to speak. You can join quietly if you prefer.

What to expect

  • parent peer support sessions
  • practical information workshops
  • programme development sessions
  • cross-sector briefings
  • local coffee sessions (SA5)

Some sessions are supportive. Some are more practical. Some help shape future work.

You can join whichever feels right for you.

About our community

Our parent carer community began in 2013 and now includes around 960 families across Wales.

Many people come to a session not knowing what to expect and stay because it feels different from other spaces.
