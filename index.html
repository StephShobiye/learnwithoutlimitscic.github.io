Learn Without Limits CIC is a parent-led organisation supporting families navigating Additional Learning Needs (ALN) in Wales.

Supporting families across Wales since 2013, we provide practical guidance, community insight, and clear navigation pathways through complex systems.

What’s happening now

Learn Without Limits CIC is already delivering support across Wales without core funding.

  • Regular online parent support sessions running across Wales
  • Cross-sector briefings bringing together education, health and community voices
  • 100+ practical guidance articles published for ALN families across Wales
  • Active parent/carer community with around 960 members
